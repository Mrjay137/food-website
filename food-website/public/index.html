Our Story

Born from a Passion for Pure Food

Verdura started as a small kitchen garden in 2009. Our founders believed that the most memorable meals are those made with ingredients grown with love and harvested at their peak.

Today, we partner with over 30 local farms and grow 40% of our herbs and microgreens on-site. Every dish tells the story of the land it came from.
